Temperature Analysis

This scatter plot shows that temperature does increase as the location nears the equator at 0 degrees latitude. I was surprised to see that the points created nearly a perfect bell curve with temperatures fairly evenly distributed throughout the world. This plot shows that May 2018 was a mild month for temperatures with the majority of temperatures grouping in the 60-80 degree F range.
